How it works

Three steps. No retraining.

01

Record

One button at the start of the consultation. Voice capture runs in the background while you talk to the patient.

02

Structure

OpenAI Whisper transcribes, Claude Haiku extracts the clinical fields: concerns, goals, BPE, occlusal class, treatment options.

03

Sign & move

You read the note, edit anything you want, and sign. Consent and estimate auto-populate from the same data.

Limits we're honest about

What this isn't.

Honest AI is a transcriptionist, not a clinician

It captures and structures what you said. It does not diagnose. It does not recommend treatment. You read everything before signing.

Honest Accuracy depends on dictation

If you mumble, the AI will too. We don't claim accuracy benchmarks because they'd be artificial — the real number is "how clearly do you dictate".

Honest Not a medical device

SmileOS is a workflow tool, not a CE-marked medical device. We don't make diagnostic claims and the consultation note never substitutes for your clinical judgment.

Honest Network-dependent

Voice processing happens server-side. If the practice WiFi is down, you can still capture audio locally and process it once back online.
