FAQ

The questions dentists actually ask.

Does this replace my dental indemnity requirements?

No. SmileOS is a workflow tool, not a clinical decision-maker. Your indemnity arrangements continue to apply exactly as before.

What if the AI mis-hears me?

You're reading every note before signing. If the AI mis-hears, you edit. Audit log records every change.

Is the patient's voice stored?

Audio is processed and discarded — we keep the transcript, not the recording. Founder cohort can opt out of any audio retention entirely.

Can I use it in different consult types?

Today it's tuned for cosmetic and orthodontic consults. Restorative and general dentistry templates are on the roadmap.
