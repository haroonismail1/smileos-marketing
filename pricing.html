Pricing FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

Is the 60-day free trial really no card?

Yes. We don't ask for a card until you've decided SmileOS is worth paying for. If it isn't, walk away — no auto-charge, no friction.

What does "founder pricing locked in for life" mean?

Whatever rate you start on stays your rate, for the lifetime of your account. When we move to public pricing, founder accounts don't move with it.

What happens if my practice grows mid-trial?

Add clinicians as you go. We'll only switch you to Practice tier billing once your trial ends, and only if you choose to subscribe.

Do you charge per clinician or per practice?

Per practice, with a clinician cap per tier (Solo: 1, Practice: 5, Group: unlimited). No per-seat surprises mid-month.

What's NOT included today?

We're honest about this: multi-site rollups, deep ClinCheck/Invisalign integration, and native mobile apps are on the roadmap, not in the box today. See Trust for the full "what's true now vs what's coming" breakdown.

Can I see a contract before signing?

Yes. Email the founder and we'll send the current Practice agreement. We're still maturing the formal DPA template — happy to talk it through before you commit.
