Orthodontics was once considered low risk. GDC clinical advisors now confirm this is no longer the case — with a sharp and sustained rise in cases year on year.

The complaint that ends a career rarely comes from a bad dentist

It comes from a good dentist who didn't track their patients closely enough. Who didn't document a conversation properly. Who didn't flag a missed appointment. These aren't clinical failures — they're system failures.

The three most commonly proven charges at GDC hearings for orthodontic cases are: poor clinical assessment records, inadequate consent documentation, and missed patient reviews. SmileOS is built to prevent all three — automatically.
