Common questions

Founder access, straight answers.

What does "founder access" actually mean?

It means you're one of the first 50 UK practices using SmileOS. You get a direct line to me (Haroon, the founder), input into what we build next, and your founder pricing locked in for the lifetime of your account.

Why no credit card up-front?

Because asking for a card before you've used the product is how you end up with churn on day 61. I'd rather you try it for two months, decide it's worth paying for, and then we sort billing.

What happens after the 60 days?

I'll get in touch before the trial ends. If it's working for your practice, you move onto founder pricing. If it isn't, you walk away — we'll help you export your data.

Who built this and why should I trust it?

I'm Haroon — a UK GDC-registered clinical director. I'm building SmileOS because the consultation workflow in my own practice was broken, and existing tools didn't fix it. You can email me at any time.

Is my patient data safe?

Your practice data is tenant-scoped on every API request, hosted on UK/EU infrastructure (Supabase), and isolated from other practices. We're still maturing the formal compliance surface (DPA, DPIA, ICO registration) — happy to talk through where we're at on a call before you sign anything.

What if it's not the right fit?

That's fine. Cancel any time during the trial — no card, no commitment. If you'd cancel after that, give 30 days' notice and we'll help you export everything.
