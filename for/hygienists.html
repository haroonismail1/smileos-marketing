Today

What hygienists get from SmileOS right now.

Even before dedicated hygienist accounts ship, the platform improves how your work fits with the dentist-led consultation.

See the consult before the appointment

Open the patient's case and read what the dentist captured — BPE plan, treatment goals, hygiene flags. No more "ask the dentist later".

Pre-treatment plan visible

The dentist's pre-treatment instructions (scale-and-polish, OHI, perio review) sit on the case file. You see them in context, not in a paper note.

Visit-by-visit history

Every consult, consent, estimate, and treatment-plan event on the case is in one timeline. Continuity of care, instantly.

Coming next

Dedicated hygienist workflows — shaped by the founder cohort.

These are on the immediate roadmap. If you join as part of the cohort, you have a direct say in how they work.

Coming Q3

Hygienist accounts & permissions

Dedicated login with hygienist-scoped views — your patient list, your visits, your notes. Read-write where it matters, read-only where it shouldn't.

Coming Q3

BPE-driven visit planning

The dentist sets the BPE plan; you see it as a queue. Scale-and-polish, OHI, perio review — actioned and signed off in one place.

Coming Q3

Hygienist voice notes

The same AI voice-to-note flow the dentists love, tuned for hygiene visits. Less typing, more conversation.

Coming Q4

Patient education library

Hygiene-specific videos and visual guides you can send patients before or after a visit. Better adherence, fewer no-shows.

Coming Q4

Recall built around your diary

Recall logic that respects hygiene rotation, not just dentist appointments. Less double-booking, better attendance.

Coming Q4

Audit trail for hygiene visits

The same audit-log discipline applied to hygiene workflows — defensible records of every visit, every recommendation, every refusal.
