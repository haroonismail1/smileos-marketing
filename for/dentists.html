Dentist FAQ

Real questions, straight answers.

Does the AI replace clinical judgment?

No. It assists with capture and structure. You read every note, edit anything you want, and sign. The AI is a transcriptionist with a clinical vocabulary — not a clinician.

What about indemnity?

SmileOS isn't a medical device — it's a workflow tool. Your indemnity arrangements continue to apply. We're happy to introduce you to the dental defence union conversations we're already having.

Will my notes hold up to GDC scrutiny?

The notes are yours — you sign them. The audit log records every edit. We've structured the workflow so that what you sign is defensible because it's a verbatim record of what you decided.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You own it. CSV export is available today, full structured export on request.
