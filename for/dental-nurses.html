Today

What changes for nurses on day one.

Even before dedicated nurse accounts ship, the team-wide case file means your day looks different.

Consent in two clicks

The dentist signs the consultation note. You hit "send consent" — patient gets it on their phone, signs in-chair or at home.

One case file, every detail

Patient demographics, consult notes, consent status, estimate, payments — all on one screen, all up-to-date.

Action list, not chaos

The dashboard tells you exactly which cases need follow-up today — unsigned consents, unverified payments, missing approvals.

Coming next

Nurse-specific workflows on the way.

Coming Q3

Nurse accounts

Dedicated login. Permissions scoped to nurse responsibilities — case file management, consent dispatch, estimate sending. No clinical sign-off rights.

Coming Q3

Pre-appointment checklist

Auto-generated checklist for each upcoming case — what's been signed, what's missing, what to chase before the patient sits down.

Coming Q4

Materials & setup tracking

Track which materials, instruments and setup each treatment needs. Lower the bar for new nurses, raise the floor for everyone.

Coming Q4

Cross-cover handover

One-screen handover for locum nurses or holiday cover. Patient, history, what's next — nothing falls through the gap.

Coming Q4

Sterilisation audit log

Tied to the case file — defensible record of decontamination, instrument lot numbers, autoclave cycle IDs. The kind of audit the CQC actually wants.

Coming 2027

Patient mobile tasks

Send the patient a list of things to do before their visit — forms, photos, payment confirmation. They tick it off; you don't chase.
